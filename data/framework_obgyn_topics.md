# Obstetrics & Gynaecology - Year 4 Framework Topic Taxonomy

High-yield (HY) calibration: Australian end-of-medical-school MCQ level. HY = must-know core presentations and Australian-specific protocols (anti-D, NCSP, GBS, GDM screening, hypertensive disorders). MID = important but less frequently tested. LOW = niche/subspecialty depth.
