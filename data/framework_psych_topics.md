# Psychiatry Framework - Topic Taxonomy

Derived from the 2026 Year 4 Psychiatry Framework. Topic numbering reflects the source framework's five-topic structure. Tags: `[HY]` high-yield for end-of-Australian-MD MCQs, `[MID]` mid-yield, `[LOW]` low-yield.
